The Journey Begins

Defining roles, understanding topics, and launching the research roadmap.

On December 8th, we officially launched the 2026 cycle with an energetic Kick-off meeting. It was inspiring to see the faces that will shape the future of our industry. This year, we are bridging the gap between young professionals and senior experts to tackle the most pressing challenges in space project management.

Key Takeaways & Policy Reminders

  • Attendance: As per the policy shared, delegates must attend at least 70% of live training sessions to receive the Certificate of Completion.
  • Get Started: Don't wait! Contact your OC Representatives and start your initial topic meetings immediately. There is never enough time!
